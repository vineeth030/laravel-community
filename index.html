Event Schedule

Here is our event schedule

  • January 20

The event will be an opportunity for beginners, intermediate, and advanced Laravel users, as well as anyone interested in web development using Laravel and its ecosystem. It will feature talks, networking, and discussions on various topics related to Laravel, such as best practices, testing, performance, security, and more. The event will also provide opportunities for attendees to showcase their skills and get feedback. The event is organized by Laravel Community, Kerala, a communityled and community-driven initiative to promote Laravel in kerala. The event is a mustattend for anyone who wants to level up their Laravel skills, meet new friends, and have fun..

Event Venue

Event venue location info and gallery

Athulya Auditorium, Infopark Campus, Kochi

Athulya Auditorium, located within the vibrant Infopark Campus in Kochi, provides a dynamic setting for events and gatherings. Nestled in the heart of the bustling IT hub, this modern venue offers state-of-the-art facilities and a conducive environment for meetings, conferences, and community events. With its contemporary design and strategic location, Athulya Auditorium is the ideal space for fostering collaboration and knowledge-sharing within the tech community.
